About




Welcome to Blushing Beat!

Blush to the Beat is a site where we report beauty news, review the newest makeup, and post some incredible looks based on the season.

I am a 20-year-old sophomore student at Lipscomb University in Nashville, Tennessee. Although my major is in Web Development, I still remain passionate in my love for makeup. I still purchase it, wear it often, and love to express myself with the simplest or the most complex of looks. I hope you find something useful from this website!

- Afua Mensah, founder
