Zara announces launch of new makeup collection for Christmas

Reposted from the Independent

Zara has announced the release of a brand new cosmetics line just in time for your Christmas shopping.

The makeup range has been inspired by the dramatic looks devised by Vogue beauty editor-at-large Pat McGrath for the Zara autumn/winter 2018 campaign, which was shot by Steven Meisel.

Zara has followed in the footsteps of other high street retailers such as Topshop and & Other Stories in launching a makeup line that will likely prove popular among consumers.

The collection by the Spanish retailer features a range of pigmented, matte lipsticks and matte liquid lipsticks.

Zara explains the inspiration behind the bold lipstick line, highlighting the importance of ensuring that the products in the range are of a high quality and reasonably priced.

“During the initial stages of creation Zara sought expert knowledge and guidance from some of the most influential, experienced beauty professionals in the industry,” the retailer says.

“This has resulted in the production of long-lasting, easy to use, matte lipstick shades.

“With the new addition of this beauty line Zara aims to provide top quality, innovative, fun and affordable products.”

The Zara Ultimatte collection is going to be available to purchase from 5 December exclusively from the Zara website.

The 12 matte lipsticks and eight matte liquid lipsticks will be available to buy individually, while some select shades will also be on offer in a limited edition three-piece lip kit.
